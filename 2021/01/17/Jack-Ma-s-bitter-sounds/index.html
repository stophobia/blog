Jack Ma's bitter sounds

The most difficult people in the world to work with are the poor.

When giving them freedom, they say it is a trap.

When talking about small businesses, they say it don’t make much money.

When talking about a big business, they complain that they have no money.

When proposing to try a new one, they excuse that they have no experience.

When proposing a traditional business, they fear that competition is fierce.

When talking about a new business model, they complains that is a multi-level.

When talking about running a store together, they say that they have no freedom.

When talking start a new business, they say that there are no experts.

They have something in common.

They like to hear opinions from hopeless friends, enjoy asking Google and sites, think more than a university professor, but do less than a blind man.

Ask them what you can do.

They won’t be able to answer.

Poor people fail because of one common behavior.

Their life waits and ends.

Are you poor man?
